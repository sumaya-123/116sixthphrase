89. Please write a program to generate all sentences where subject is in ["I",
"You"] and verb is in ["Play", "Love"] and the object is in
["Hockey","Football"].




code:



subjects=["I", "You"]
verbs=["Play", "Love"]
objects=["Hockey","Football"]

for sub in subjects:
    for verb in verbs:
        for obj in objects:
            print("{} {} {}".format(sub,verb,obj))
